Iconic Locations

Taj Mahal

Taj Mahal: Symbol of eternal love in Agra, India. Majestic marble masterpiece. One of the Iconic places to visit in India.

Hawa Mahal

Hawa Mahal: Jaipur's iconic 'Palace of Winds,' known for its unique honeycomb facade and rich historical significance.

Rumi Gate

Rumi Gate: Iconic 17th-century landmark in Lucknow, India. Rich Mughal architecture, named after poet Rumi.

Jantar Mantar

Jantar Mantar: Ancient astronomical observatory in India, showcasing impressive architectural precision.
